CONNECT - IMPORTANT! Turn on the controller FIRST, and then press 'Connect' in the app to connect to Sumato.

VR MODE - Control Sumato using VR Headset and the controller. Move your head according to the direction you want to see. Control Sumato's direction using the controller's arrow buttons.

STANDARD MODE - Control Sumato using your smartphone(optional) and the controller. Control Sumato's direction using the controller's arrow buttons.
